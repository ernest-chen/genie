Executive Summary

Meta's business model in 2025 remains fundamentally anchored in attention monetization—primarily through advertising across Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp, which accounts for roughly 98% of its revenue. The company has made major investments in AI, generative technologies, hardware (like smart glasses), and infrastructure expansion, signaling broader ambitions beyond social media.

Key Strengths

  • • Massive user base across multiple platforms
  • • Advanced AI and machine learning capabilities
  • • Strong advertising ecosystem and targeting
  • • Significant investment in AR/VR technologies

Key Challenges

  • • High regulatory scrutiny and privacy concerns
  • • Dependence on advertising revenue (98%)
  • • Competition from TikTok and other platforms
  • • Ongoing legal battles and antitrust probes
